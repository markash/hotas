Hot As...

Is your corporate culture hot, sizzling, blazing hot or merely mediocre or worse freezing cold? How do you find out what your employees are thinking or feeling concerning their working environment? And most importantly how do you track your progress once you start to implement changes?

Well, Hot As... tracks the anonymous opinions of your employees and then coalates the information for supervisors and managers.

  • Got an idea

    Got a super brilliant idea or excellent news then we'd like to hear from you our greatest asset. Even if you would like to say what is good or what could be improved, that is cool also. In a matter of fact we would like to hear all your comments.

  • Got a complaint

    Got a complaint or something on your chest then we'd like to hear from you our greatest asset. Even if you would like to say what is good or what could be improved, that is cool also. In a matter of fact we would like to hear all your comments.

  • Hear what others are saying

    Comment or agree on what other are saying around the virtual water. Bring ideas to the bugbears expressed by other or build on the ideas and positive feed back. Converse with your peers to improve the working environment for the benefit of all.
